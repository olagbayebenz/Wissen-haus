## 2. ABOUT US

### The Foundation's Journey
We founded Wissen-Haus Youth Empowerment Foundation with a simple yet profound mission: to bridge the skills gap in Nigeria. Our journey began as a collective effort to equip young people with the practical knowledge and global exposure they need to thrive. By combining career guidance bootcamps with the 'Opportunity Blueprint' podcast, we are creating a bridge between the classroom and the world, ensuring that every young Nigerian has the tools to achieve economic independence and make a lasting impact.

### Our Leadership
Guided by a team of dedicated professionals committed to empowering the next generation of Nigerian youth.
- **Dr. Akinola Oluwase** — Founder & CEO
- **Chidi Okoro** — Program Director
- **Isaac Adewale** — Community Lead
