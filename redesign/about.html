About the Foundation

The bridge between the classroom and the world.

We founded Wissen-Haus Youth Empowerment Foundation with a simple yet profound mission: to bridge the skills gap in Nigeria.

01 · Our Journey

The Foundation's Journey

Our journey began as a collective effort to equip young people with the practical knowledge and global exposure they need to thrive. By combining career guidance bootcamps with the Opportunity Blueprint podcast, we are creating a bridge between the classroom and the world, ensuring that every young Nigerian has the tools to achieve economic independence and make a lasting impact.

Timeline

Key milestones on the journey.

From formal registration to national ambitions, the moments that mark our growth.

Milestone 01

Foundation registered & first cohort launched

Wissen-Haus Youth Empowerment Foundation is formally registered and the first cohort of youth programmes is launched.

Milestone 02

First 100 youth reached

Completion of initial bootcamps and mentorship sessions impacting the first 100 young people.

Milestone 03

Opportunity Blueprint podcast launch

Official release of the podcast, featuring the first season focused on career pathways and global opportunities.

Milestone 04

Partnerships & institutional collaboration

Signing of key partnerships with schools, NGOs and employers to expand access and placement opportunities.

Milestone 05

National expansion & impact metrics

Expansion beyond Ibadan to multiple states, with clear metrics like total youth trained, internships secured and jobs obtained.
