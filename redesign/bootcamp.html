Trade Fair

Explore careers. Meet professionals. Find your path.

An immersive career exploration day for all secondary school students (JSS1-SS3) in Ibadan. Meet 15+ professionals from different industries, explore careers that match your interests, and discover pathways to your future.

Programme Overview

What is the Trade Fair?

Meet Real Professionals

Talk directly with people working in careers that interest you. Ask real questions about what the job is like, how they got there, and what it takes to succeed.

Explore Multiple Paths

Meet professionals from tech, business, media, healthcare, social impact, and more. Get exposed to careers you might not have known existed.

Global Exposure

Connect with international opportunities, learn about global career trends, and gain insights into the skills needed to thrive in a global economy.

For Every Secondary Student

Everyone is welcome. No matter where you are in your journey.

📚

Just Starting Out (JSS)

You're in your first years of secondary school. This is the perfect time to explore what's out there and dream big about your future.

🎯

Still Figuring It Out (SS1-SS2)

You're in the middle years and still exploring. The Trade Fair is your chance to meet real people doing real jobs and see what speaks to you.

💡

Almost There (SS3)

You're about to finish secondary school. Use this day to make connections with professionals, confirm your path, and get real advice for your next steps.

How It Works

The Trade Fair experience.

1

Registration

Express interest through our online form. Tell us about yourself and your career goals.

2

Orientation

Meet organizers and other participants. Get an overview of the professionals you'll meet and careers you'll explore today.

3

Career Booths & Conversations

Visit different career booths. Talk with professionals about their roles, how they started, challenges, and what excites them about their work.

4

Deep-Dive Sessions

Join focused workshops on careers that interest you most. Get real answers about skills needed, education paths, and how to get started.

5

Closing & Networking

Connect with professionals one-on-one. Exchange contact info, discuss next steps, and get advice on how to pursue careers you're interested in.

6

Stay Connected

Join our community to stay in touch with professionals you met, access mentorship, and get job/internship opportunities based on your interests.

Programme Details

Everything you need to know.

Duration

One full day of career exploration, networking and discovery. Typically held on Saturdays to make it easy for students to attend without missing school.

Cost

Completely FREE. We believe quality career guidance should be accessible to every young Nigerian, regardless of their background.

Schedule

9 AM - 3 PM on event day. Check-in at 8:30 AM. Full day includes booth visits, group discussions, lunch, and networking sessions.

Location

Ibadan, Nigeria. In-person event with venue and facilities to host booths, discussions, and networking. All materials, refreshments, and lunch provided.

Mentors

Learn from working professionals in tech, business, education, media, and social impact. Our mentors are committed to your success.

Support

Expert organizers on hand throughout the day to help guide you, answer questions, and connect you with professionals. Post-event mentorship available too.
