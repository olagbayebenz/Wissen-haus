Career Bootcamp

Build the skills that matter.

Programme Overview

What is the Career Bootcamp?

Intensive & Practical

Structured sessions combining classroom learning with real-world project work. Develop skills you can use immediately in your career journey.

Mentorship Network

Learn directly from professionals working in tech, business, media, and social impact. Get guidance on careers, education and personal development.

Global Exposure

Connect with international opportunities, learn about global career trends, and gain insights into the skills needed to thrive in a global economy.

Who Should Apply

The ideal bootcamp participant.

📚

Secondary Students

Currently in JS3, SS1, or SS2. You have the curiosity to explore career options and the drive to build practical skills.

🎯

Career Explorers

You're unsure about your next steps but want guidance. You're open to learning new skills and meeting professionals in different fields.

💡

Future Leaders

You want to make an impact and aren't sure how. You're interested in entrepreneurship, social innovation, or building a meaningful career.

What You'll Learn

Core skills for career success.

1

Career Clarity

Explore diverse career paths, understand your strengths and interests, and build a realistic 5-year career plan aligned with your goals.

2

Professional Skills

Master CV writing, interview preparation, professional communication, networking, and personal branding to stand out to employers.

3

Technical Skills

Gain practical hands-on skills in digital literacy, coding basics, digital marketing, or other high-demand technical competencies.

4

Soft Skills

Develop communication, teamwork, problem-solving, time management, and leadership skills that employers value in every industry.

5

Global Perspective

Learn about international opportunities, global career trends, and how Nigerian talent can compete and thrive in the global marketplace.

6

Entrepreneurship

For those interested in starting your own venture—learn the basics of business planning, pitching ideas, and building a sustainable startup.

How It Works

The bootcamp journey.

1

Registration

Express interest through our online form. Tell us about yourself and your career goals.

2

Orientation

Meet our mentor team, connect with fellow participants, and get clarity on the bootcamp structure and expectations.

3

Learning Modules

Participate in intensive workshops, mentorship sessions, and hands-on projects. Learn directly from working professionals.

4

Project Work

Apply your learning to real-world projects. Work in teams, present your work, and get feedback from mentors and peers.

5

Completion & Recognition

Graduate from the bootcamp with a certificate of completion, LinkedIn recommendations, and lasting connections with mentors.

6

Alumni Network

Join our growing alumni network. Access career resources, job opportunities, and continue your mentorship journey.

Programme Details

Everything you need to know.

Duration

4-6 weeks of intensive learning, typically held during school holidays. Flexible schedule to accommodate your school commitments.

Cost

Completely FREE. We believe quality career guidance should be accessible to every young Nigerian, regardless of their background.

Schedule

Sessions held in the evenings and weekends to fit around your school schedule. You choose the format that works best for you.

Location

Based in Ibadan, Nigeria. In-person sessions with optional hybrid arrangements. All materials and resources provided.

Mentors

Learn from working professionals in tech, business, education, media, and social impact. Our mentors are committed to your success.

Support

Dedicated support throughout your bootcamp journey. We're here to help you overcome challenges and maximize your learning.
