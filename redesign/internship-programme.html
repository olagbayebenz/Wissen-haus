TypeInternship
Duration3-6 months

About This Internship

Support Trade Fair planning, participant onboarding and impact tracking across our programmes. You'll work closely with our operations team to ensure every programme runs smoothly and delivers maximum value to our members.

What You'll Do

  • Assist with Trade Fair logistics and participant recruitment
  • Help coordinate mentor-participant matching and scheduling
  • Track programme metrics and participant progress
  • Create participant resources and training materials
  • Support post-Trade Fair follow-up and impact documentation
  • Attend and support live Trade Fair sessions

What You'll Learn

  • How to design and execute scalable educational programmes
  • Project and operations management skills
  • Data tracking and impact measurement
  • How nonprofits bridge the skills gap
  • Leadership and youth development practices

Who We're Looking For

  • Currently enrolled student or recent graduate (any field)
  • Strong organizational and communication skills
  • Attention to detail and ability to manage multiple tasks
  • Passion for youth empowerment and education
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced nonprofit environment
  • Based in or willing to relocate to Ibadan

Benefits

  • Hands-on experience in nonprofit operations
  • Mentorship from experienced programme leaders
  • Certificate of completion and LinkedIn recommendation
  • Networking opportunities with 15,000+ young professionals
  • Flexible schedule during academic periods
