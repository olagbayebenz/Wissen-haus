Frequently Asked Questions

How long will the review process take?

Our team typically reviews all applications within 5-7 business days. We'll contact qualified candidates to schedule an interview. If we don't contact you after this period, we encourage you to apply for other opportunities.

Will I receive updates on my application status?

Yes! You'll receive email updates at key milestones: (1) confirmation of receipt, (2) interview invitation (if selected), and (3) final decision. We respect your time and will keep you informed throughout the process.

What if I have questions about the role?

Great question! You can reach out to us at info@wissenhaus.org with any questions about the position, our organization, or the application process. We're happy to help!

Can I apply for multiple roles?

Absolutely! If you're interested in multiple roles, feel free to submit separate applications for each position. Just make sure to tailor your responses to highlight relevant skills for each role.

What if I don't hear back?

If you haven't heard from us within 10 business days, please check your spam folder or reach out to info@wissenhaus.org. We're committed to acknowledging every application.
