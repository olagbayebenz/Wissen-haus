Community Manager

TypeVolunteer
LocationRemote
CommitmentFlexible

About This Role

Nurture our online community, moderate discussions, and keep the Opportunity Hub fresh with new opportunities for our members. You'll be the heart of our digital community, fostering connections and supporting members on their journeys.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor and moderate community discussions and forums
  • Curate and share job, internship, scholarship, and grant opportunities
  • Respond to member inquiries and provide support
  • Facilitate connections between members and mentors
  • Track community engagement metrics and feedback
  • Help maintain a positive, inclusive community culture

What We're Looking For

  • 1+ years of community management or social media experience
  • Strong written communication skills
  • Excellent customer service and conflict resolution abilities
  • Genuine passion for supporting young Nigerians
  • Comfort with community platforms and digital tools

Ready to Build Community?

If you love connecting people and fostering belonging, help us build a thriving community of young empowered Nigerians.
