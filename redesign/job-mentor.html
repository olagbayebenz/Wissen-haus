Mentor

Type Volunteer
Location Remote / Ibadan
Commitment Flexible

About This Role

Guide JS3-SS1 students through career choices and personal growth by sharing your professional journey. As a mentor, you'll be the bridge between academic learning and real-world opportunity—helping young Nigerians navigate career decisions, build confidence, and access global opportunities.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide 1-on-1 mentorship to assigned students (1-2 hours per month)
  • Help students explore career pathways aligned with their interests and strengths
  • Share insights from your professional journey and industry experience
  • Support students in building resumes, LinkedIn profiles, and interview skills
  • Encourage participation in bootcamps, competitions and scholarship opportunities
  • Attend optional monthly community calls and mentor gatherings

What We're Looking For

  • 5+ years of professional experience in any field
  • Passion for mentoring young people and closing the skills gap
  • Excellent communication and listening skills
  • Willingness to share your story and learn from your mentees
  • Reliability and commitment to consistent mentorship
  • Based in Nigeria or willing to mentor via remote/video calls

What You'll Gain

  • Meaningful impact on a young person's career trajectory
  • Access to our mentor community and professional network
  • Continuing education and mentorship training
  • Certificate of recognition for your contribution
  • Monthly newsletters and community updates

Ready to Make an Impact?

If you're passionate about empowering young Nigerians and have valuable experience to share, we'd love to have you join our mentorship network. Your guidance could change someone's life.
