Technical Trainer

TypeVolunteer
LocationIbadan
CommitmentFlexible

About This Role

Teach coding, digital marketing, or vocational skills to eager Trade Fair participants and help close the skills gap. You'll be shaping the future of young Nigerian professionals by equipping them with in-demand technical skills.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and deliver hands-on training curriculum (coding, digital marketing, vocational skills)
  • Guide Trade Fair cohorts through practical projects and real-world scenarios
  • Create learning materials and resources for students
  • Provide feedback and mentorship to help students progress
  • Collaborate with other trainers and program coordinators

What We're Looking For

  • 3+ years of experience in coding, digital marketing, or your area of expertise
  • Strong teaching and communication skills
  • Ability to break down complex concepts into simple, understandable lessons
  • Passion for youth development and closing the skills gap
  • Patience and willingness to work with beginners

Ready to Train the Next Generation?

If you have technical expertise and love teaching, join us in empowering Nigeria's youth with job-ready skills.
