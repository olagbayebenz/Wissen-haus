FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay for courses?

We offer both free and premium courses. Our Soft Skills course is free for all members. The other 4 courses require a £10/year membership.

Can I download certificates?

Yes! After completing all modules in any course, you'll earn a certificate that you can download as PDF or add directly to your LinkedIn profile.

How long does each course take?

Each course has 10 modules, averaging 40 minutes per module. You can learn at your own pace—there's no time limit.

What if I don't pass a module quiz?

You can retake module quizzes as many times as needed. You need 80% to pass and move to the next module.
