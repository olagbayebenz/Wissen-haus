The Journey

Before Wissen-Haus, I believed success meant leaving Nigeria. Everyone around me talked about "japa"—relocating to the US, Europe, or UK. I felt limited by my circumstances and thought good opportunities only existed elsewhere.

The Trade Fair exposed me to a different reality. I learned about global career opportunities, remote work, and how to position myself competitively on the international stage without leaving Nigeria. I skipped the expensive relocation and instead invested in upskilling.

I worked with mentors on my professional brand, improved my technical skills, and learned to network effectively online. Within a year, I landed a remote role with a US tech company, managing their African operations. Now I'm leading a team of 10+ across different countries, all while living in Lagos.

The best part? I'm earning competitive international salaries, maintaining my social connections, and serving my community. I'm proof that you don't need to leave to succeed globally. You just need the right mindset, skills, and network—all of which Wissen-Haus provided.

Advice to Young Professionals

"You don't need to leave Nigeria to succeed globally. What you need are the right skills, a strong professional brand, and access to global networks. Build your skills locally but think globally. Remote work is real and it's a game-changer for Nigerian professionals."
